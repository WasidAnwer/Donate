Questions Or Concerns on you Mind?

What is the purpose of this foundation? +

Our foundation is dedicated to providing charitable aid in accordance with Islamic values. We focus on supporting communities in need through zakat, sadaqah, and other charity efforts. Our projects include food relief, education, healthcare, and emergency assistance.

Is the foundation registered as a non-profit? +

Yes, we are a registered non-profit organization, which means we operate solely for charitable purposes and comply with local and international non-profit regulations.

Are donations tax-deductible? +

In many countries, donations to our foundation are tax-deductible. Please check with your local tax authority or consult a tax advisor to confirm eligibility in your region.

How does the foundation distribute zakat? +

Zakat is distributed strictly according to Islamic guidelines. We ensure that zakat funds are allocated to eligible recipients, including the poor, orphans, and individuals facing financial hardship. We maintain transparent records to verify zakat compliance.

What types of donations do you accept? +

We accept various forms of donations, including zakat, sadaqah, and general charity contributions. You can donate via credit card, debit card, bank transfer, and, in some cases, cryptocurrency.

Is my donation secure? +

Absolutely. We use secure online payment systems to protect your financial information. Our website and payment processors follow industry-standard security protocols.

Will I receive a donation receipt? +

Yes, you will receive an electronic receipt immediately after making an online donation. For offline donations, please contact us, and we’ll provide a receipt.

Can I see how my donation is used? +

We provide regular updates through our website and newsletters to show the impact of your donations. Donors also receive periodic reports with photos, stories, and data on project outcomes.

How do you ensure transparency with donations? +

We maintain complete financial transparency by publishing annual reports and impact summaries. These reports detail where donations are spent and the specific projects funded.

How do I contact the foundation for further questions? +

You can reach us through our contact page, which provides our email, phone number, and social media links. We’re here to answer any questions you may have.

About NextStep

At NextStep, we believe that every individual has the right to a life of dignity and hope. Guided by Islamic principles of charity and compassion, we are dedicated to empowering communities worldwide. Through your generous support, we provide essential aid, including food, clean water, healthcare, and education, to those who need it most.
